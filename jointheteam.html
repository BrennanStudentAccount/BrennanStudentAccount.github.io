If your on this page you are most likely curious or wanting to join the Staff Team. If thats the case your in luck as Lunar / Pyronic are almost always hiring. We like to have more people that can learn from others and make all the servers a more enjoyable place for all to be. If you apply or get interviewed and get accepted to be a staff member then its your lucky day as you get to be a part of a large community. As a trainee you don't get as many permissions as the other staff members, but if you even put a little bit of time into staffing you will climb the ranks at a decent speed and make sure to be the top and the higher you go the more decisions you can make for the better of the community.
