Lunar Network is a up and coming Unturned Creative RP Server. We have state of the art features that you can learn more about in the Discord Server! We have an active staff team and playerbase and are looking to become one of the Top Creative Servers. We strive to be better than the other servers that have messed up in the past. We know that not everyone or everything can be perfect so we strive to make it right and take our mistakes & others' mistakes to learn from them. We accept any and all players to the community, even previous rule breakers to hope that they can find a server that they like. The Staff Team is motivated to make it an enjoyable experience for players of all ages.
